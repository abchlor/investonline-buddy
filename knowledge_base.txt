### KYC (Know Your Customer)
**What is KYC?**
KYC is a one-time verification process for investing in mutual funds in India. It confirms your identity and address as per SEBI regulations.

**e-KYC Process:**
1. Enter PAN and Aadhaar details
2. Receive OTP on registered mobile
3. Verify OTP
4. Digital signature captured
5. KYC verified instantly (usually within minutes)

**Required Documents:**
- PAN Card (mandatory)
- Aadhaar Card (for e-KYC)
- Bank Proof: Cancelled cheque or bank statement
- Photograph (for manual KYC)
- Address Proof (if Aadhaar not used): Passport, Voter ID, Driver's License, or utility bill

**KYC Processing Time:**
- e-KYC: Instant to 24 hours
- Manual KYC: 3-5 business days

## INVESTMENT TYPES

### Systematic Investment Plan (SIP)
**What is SIP?**
SIP allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly (monthly/quarterly) in mutual funds. It's a disciplined approach with benefits like rupee cost averaging.

**Starting SIP:**
1. Complete registration and KYC
2. Choose a mutual fund scheme
3. Select SIP amount (minimum ₹500) and frequency
4. Set up auto-debit mandate
5. Choose start date

**SIP Benefits:**
- Disciplined investing habit
- Rupee cost averaging
- Power of compounding
- Flexibility to pause/stop
- No market timing needed

### Lumpsum Investment
Invest a one-time amount in mutual funds. Suitable for:
- Windfall gains (bonus, inheritance)
- Long-term goals
- When you have idle funds

## MUTUAL FUND CATEGORIES

### Equity Funds
**Best for:** Long-term wealth creation (7+ years)
**Risk:** High
**Examples:** Large Cap, Mid Cap, Small Cap, Flexi Cap, Sectoral
**Top Performers:** Check "Top Equity Funds" section on website

### Debt Funds
**Best for:** Stable returns, short-to-medium term goals
**Risk:** Low to Moderate
**Examples:** Liquid, Short Duration, Corporate Bond, Gilt
**Returns:** 6-8% annually

### Hybrid Funds
**Best for:** Balanced risk-reward
**Risk:** Moderate
**Mix:** Combination of equity and debt
**Examples:** Aggressive Hybrid (65-80% equity), Conservative Hybrid (25-40% equity)

### Tax Saver Funds (ELSS)
**Best for:** Tax savings under Section 80C
**Lock-in:** 3 years (shortest among tax-saving options)
**Tax Benefit:** Up to ₹1.5 lakh deduction
**Risk:** Moderate to High (equity-oriented)

### International Funds
**Best for:** Geographical diversification
**Risk:** Moderate to High
**Invests in:** Global markets (US, Europe, Asia)

## COMMON USER QUESTIONS

**Q: Is my money safe?**
A: Mutual fund investments are regulated by SEBI. Your units are held in your name with the fund house. InvestOnline.in is a SEBI-registered platform.

**Q: Can I invest without KYC?**
A: No, KYC is mandatory as per SEBI regulations before investing in mutual funds.

**Q: What is the minimum investment?**
A: SIP minimum: ₹500/month. Lumpsum minimum: Varies by scheme (₹1,000-₹5,000)

**Q: How do I track my investments?**
A: Login to your account → Dashboard shows portfolio value, returns, holdings

**Q: Can I stop my SIP anytime?**
A: Yes, SIPs are flexible. You can pause, stop, or modify them anytime (except during ELSS 3-year lock-in).

**Q: How are returns calculated?**
A: Returns shown as CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate) or absolute returns for periods less than 1 year.

**Q: What is NAV?**
A: Net Asset Value - the price per unit of a mutual fund, calculated daily after market close.

**Q: Are returns guaranteed?**
A: No, mutual funds are market-linked investments. Past performance doesn't guarantee future returns.
